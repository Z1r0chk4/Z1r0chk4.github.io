OUR HISTORY

To achieve the maximum result in weight loss, it is important to use an integrated approach. The training program for fat burning will help to form the body relief, remove unnecessary fatty deposits. For intensive loads, the conditions of a sports hall are more suitable, moderate ones can be performed at home. Everything is determined strictly individually.

Classes are based on usual physical exercises, aerobics, respiratory gymnastics (who have no problems with gynecology and cardiovascular system, if there is no epilepsy, hernias, cysts, heart problems - body flex, oxidase), morning run, strength training. Jumping on the rope is very suitable. A separate place is occupied by cardio loads - a key aspect in the implementation of the weight loss plan.
